Recommendations on Diabetic Diet

Appropriate dietary management not only effectively maintains blood glucose at or close to a normal level but also helps prevent or delay the complications of diabetes, such as diabetic foot, diabetic eye disease and diabetic kidney disease.

Key Dietary Recommendations

  • Maintain a balanced diet with regular meals and controlled portion sizes.
  • Eat more high-fiber foods such as vegetables, fruits, whole wheat bread, and brown rice.
  • Use healthy cooking methods like steaming, poaching, or boiling instead of frying.
  • Reduce high-fat foods like fried items, whole milk, and cakes.
  • Avoid sugary foods and drinks. Opt for natural sweeteners in moderation if necessary.
  • Choose foods low in fat and sodium. Always check nutrition labels.
