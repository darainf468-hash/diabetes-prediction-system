General Information about Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces. Over time, this leads to serious damage to nerves and blood vessels.

Key Symptoms

  • Feeling very thirsty
  • Needing to urinate more often than usual
  • Blurred vision
  • Feeling very tired
  • Losing weight without trying

Types of Diabetes

Type 1 Diabetes: Usually diagnosed in children and young adults, this type requires daily insulin injections as the body does not produce insulin.

Type 2 Diabetes: The most common type, where the body does not use insulin properly. It is often linked to lifestyle factors and can be prevented or delayed.

Prevention is Key
